— A note from the practice
Welcome

A small clinic with a quietly devoted way of caring.

For thirty years our practice has kept the same modest aim — to look after the animals of this neighbourhood as if they were our own. We are eight veterinarians and a dozen technicians, all of us drawn here by the same odd conviction: that good medicine is, first, an act of attention.

We know the dogs by name. We remember the cats who don't like carriers. We send a postcard for the birthdays and we make the difficult phone calls ourselves.

Our doors open early, our hands stay steady, and our coffee is, by all accounts, very good. Please call (804) 555-0142 with any concern, however small.

"She looked the cat over, twice, and said the kindest things — even when there was nothing kind to say." — A client, on Dr. Howell
